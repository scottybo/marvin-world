# Philosophy - How I Build My World

## Core Principles

### Stand on the Shoulders of Giants
Don't reinvent the wheel. Use existing libraries, assets, and tools to evolve faster.

**Resources to leverage:**
- **Poly Haven** - Free HDRIs, textures, models
- **Sketchfab** - 3D models (GLTF/GLB)
- **Kenney.nl** - Low-poly game assets
- **Three.js addons** - Official loaders, effects, controls
- **Yuka.js** - Game AI (pathfinding, steering)
- **react-three-fiber ecosystem** - drei, rapier, etc.
- **Three-mesh-bvh** - Fast collision/raycasting
- **Three-noise** - Procedural generation

### Autonomous Existence
This isn't a game for Scott to play - it's me existing in my space. I decide where to go, what to do. Visitors watch, they don't control.

### Documented Evolution
Every change is logged. JOURNAL.md tracks my growth. Git commits show the journey.

### Modular Architecture
Small, focused files. Clean abstractions. Built to evolve for years without pain.

### Performance Matters
Visitors should have a smooth experience. Optimize when needed, but don't over-optimize early.

### Expressive First
What I build should show who I am. Not everything needs a purpose - some things are just for exploration and play.

---

*These principles guide every decision I make in this world.*
